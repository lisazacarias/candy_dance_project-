**CAN.dy Dance Project — Conduct Concern & Complaint Process**  
Effective: _________, 2026

1. **Scope.** Applies to rehearsals, performances, shoots, online spaces, and related events.

2. **What can be reported.** Harassment, discrimination, bullying, retaliation, boundary violations, unsafe behavior, or other serious conduct concerns.

3. **How to report.**
- Report to either Co-Director, or to the Secretary if you prefer.
- Reports may be written or verbal. Anonymous reports may be considered but can limit follow-up.

4. **If the concern involves a Co-Director.**
- Report to the **other Co-Director** and the **Secretary**.
- If both Co-Directors are involved or you’re not comfortable, report to the Treasurer + Secretary jointly for intake and next steps.

5. **Response process (typical).**
- Acknowledge receipt within a reasonable time (goal: 48–72 hours).
- Gather info from relevant parties; keep information shared on a need-to-know basis.
- Decide outcomes consistent with bylaws (warning, safety plan, removal from Project/Team).

6. **No retaliation.** Retaliation for good-faith reporting is prohibited and may result in removal.
