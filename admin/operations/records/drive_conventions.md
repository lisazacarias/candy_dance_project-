**Recommended Google Drive folder convention**

Create one top-level folder:
- **CAN.dy Admin**
  - **01 Projects & Events** (all bookings + projects)
  - **02 Finance** (statements, reports)
  - **03 Governance** (bylaws, policies, minutes)

For each WildApricot Event, create a matching Drive folder under **01 Projects & Events**:

**Folder name format (best for sorting):**
- `YYYY-MM-DD — [VENUE] — [EVENT TYPE] — [PROJECT NAME]`
Examples:
- `2026-03-14 — Tabard Theatre — Performance — Sugar Rush`
- `2026-02-05 — Dance Academy USA — Rehearsal Block — Sugar Rush`
- `2026-04-10 — 7th Street Bar — Performance — Candy Cabaret`

Inside each event folder, use consistent subfolders:
- `01 Contract & Insurance`
- `02 Budget & Payments`
- `03 Marketing & Media`
- `04 Logistics`
- `05 Reports & Closeout`

File names (keep consistent):
- `Contract — [Venue] — YYYY-MM-DD.pdf`
- `Invoice — [Venue] — YYYY-MM-DD.pdf`
- `Insurance COI — [Venue] — YYYY-MM-DD.pdf`
- `Recording Permission — [Venue] — YYYY-MM-DD.pdf`
- `Budget — [Project] — v1.xlsx`
- `Closeout Summary — [Project] — YYYY-MM-DD.docx`

For `02 Finance`, keep monthly banking records from **Stanford Federal Credit Union** consistent:
- `Bank Statement — Stanford Federal Credit Union — YYYY-MM.pdf`
- `Transaction Export — Stanford Federal Credit Union — YYYY-MM.csv`

## “Create a new Event” checklist (Project Coordinator can run)
1) Create WildApricot Event using the right template (Rehearsal Block / Performance / Video Shoot).
2) Create the matching Drive folder using the naming format above.
3) Put any contract/quote emails or PDFs into `01 Contract & Insurance`.
4) Paste Drive links into WildApricot fields:
   - Contract Link
   - Recording Permission Link (if applicable)
5) Fill in Money fields + deadlines; notify Treasurer.
6) Confirm approvals:
   - Co-Director Approval #1 + #2 = Yes before committing funds/signing.
7) After the event/project, upload receipts/summary into `05 Reports & Closeout` and mark “Secretary Saved Docs = Yes”.

Effective: _________, 2026

### 1) Folder structure (reference)
Top-level: **CAN.dy Admin**  
- **01 Projects & Events**  
- **02 Finance**  
- **03 Governance**

### 2) Standard permission levels
- **Admin (Full control):** can add/remove users, change permissions.  
- **Editor:** can create/edit/delete files in assigned folders.  
- **Viewer:** read-only.

### 3) Role-based access (recommended default)

**Co-Directors**
- CAN.dy Admin: **Admin**
- 01 Projects & Events: **Editor**
- 02 Finance: **Viewer** (or Editor if you want them able to upload contracts/receipts)
- 03 Governance: **Editor**

**Treasurer**
- CAN.dy Admin: **Editor**
- 01 Projects & Events: **Viewer** (Editor allowed if Treasurer uploads receipts/contracts)
- 02 Finance: **Editor**
- 03 Governance: **Viewer** (Editor if Treasurer contributes to policies)

**Secretary**
- CAN.dy Admin: **Editor**
- 01 Projects & Events: **Editor**
- 02 Finance: **Viewer**
- 03 Governance: **Editor** (official recordkeeper)

**Project Coordinator (per-project appointment)**
- Only the relevant Project/Event folder(s) in 01 Projects & Events: **Editor**
- All other folders: **No access** (unless needed)

**Social Media Manager (per-project appointment)**
- Relevant Project/Event folder(s) → `03 Marketing & Media` subfolder: **Editor**
- All other folders: **No access** (unless needed)

**Outreach Lead (appointed role)**
- Relevant Project/Event folder(s), outreach tracker(s), or outreach materials subfolder(s): **Editor or Viewer** (as needed)
- No access to 02 Finance and 03 Governance by default

**Project Choreographer(s) (per project)**
- Relevant Project/Event folder(s) (or a “Choreography & Rehearsal Notes” subfolder you create): **Editor or Viewer** (your choice)
- No access to 02 Finance and 03 Governance by default
