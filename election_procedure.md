**CAN.dy Dance Project — Officer Election Procedure**  
Version/Adopted: _________, 2026

### A. Offices covered by elections
The elected officer positions are:
- **Treasurer** (1 seat)
- **Secretary** (1 seat)

### B. Who may vote
Only **members in good standing** may vote (per the bylaws).

### C. Who may run
Any member in good standing may run for Treasurer or Secretary, plus any additional candidate approved unanimously by both Co-Directors (per the bylaws).

### D. Election timing (annual)
1. Elections will be held **annually in November**.  
2. The Secretary will announce the election calendar at least **14 days** before voting opens.

### E. Nominations
1. The Secretary will open nominations at least **14 days** before the election closes.  
2. Nominations may be self-nominations or nominations by others (with the nominee’s consent).  
3. The Secretary will publish the candidate list and any candidate statements at least **5 days** before voting opens.

### F. Voting method
1. Voting may be conducted online (e.g., secure form) or in person.  
2. Voting will remain open for at least **48 hours**.  
3. Each eligible voter may cast one vote for Treasurer and one vote for Secretary (or abstain).

### G. Winning threshold and ties
1. The candidate receiving the highest number of votes wins the seat.  
2. **Tie:** a 48-hour runoff vote will be held. If still tied, the Co-Directors will select among the tied candidates.

### H. Transition
1. The outgoing officer(s) will transfer relevant accounts, files, keys, and records within **14 days** of results being announced.  
2. Treasurer transition must include current balances, access credentials, and a transaction report.

### I. Recordkeeping
The Secretary will retain the election notice, candidate list, voting dates, and results.
